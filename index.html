About

The Project

3D Modelling Software supports many important applications, including: creating virtual scenes for video games and movies; creating models for 3D printing and viewing 3D models of medical information. Despite the importance of these applications, the 3D user interfaces of these modelling packages have not changed much since their inception. While many alternative interfaces have been proposed, none have gained traction. In order to determine whether these interfaces have merit over the traditional ones, we are running a comparative quantitative evaluation of these interfaces against the already established paradigms. We will be evaluating the use of shadow widgets for translation and rotation as well as three rotation controllers. We aim to determine whether they allow users to perform tasks more accurately and quickly as well as whether they are more usable.
